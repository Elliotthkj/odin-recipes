Japanese Curry

The stand-out feature of a Japanese curry is its thick, rich sauce. The thickness of the sauce which can only be found in Japanese curry is supported and beloved by many. The rich and indulgent sauce mixes with rice so perfectly, you will find it difficult to stop eating.

Ingredients

  • Golden Curry mix
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Your choice of protein
    • Chicken
    • Pork
    • Shrimp
  • Your choice of vegetables
    • Carrots
    • Potatoes
    • Tofu
    • Spinach
    • (Feel free to add anything you want)
  • Cheese for topping
  • Pickled vegetables on the side if available!

Steps

  1. Wash rice and start rice cooker (don't forget to start the cooker like me)
  2. Wash and prep ALL produce
    • Dice onions and garlic
    • Cut veggies into bit-sized pieces
    • If using tofu, drain liquid and dry with paper towels
  3. Add oil or butter to large pot and saut√© onions and garlic (salt and pepper to taste as you go)
  4. Add protein and cook till 90% cooked
  5. Add veggies to pot (save tofu for later) and barely cover with water
  6. Bring to boil then lower heat to medium and let your veggies cook
  7. While veggies are cooking, add Golden Curry mix cubes until desired thickness
  8. (Add tofu once veggies are nearly done)
  9. Once veggies are cooked serve curry with rice
  10. Add any additional toppings
  11. Enjoy! (Do your best to avoid falling into the crippling addiction that is Japanese style curry)
