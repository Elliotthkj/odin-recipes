Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine. Like other pasta, spaghetti is made of milled wheat and water and sometimes enriched with vitamins and minerals. Italian spaghetti is typically made from durum wheat semolina. This recipe is for a tomato and meat sauce pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef and 1/2 lb sweet sausage
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives
  • Tomato Sauce (two 15oz cans)
  • Tomato Paste (two 6oz cans)
  • Stewed Tomatoes (one 15oz can)
  • Seasonings
    • Worchestshire sauce
    • Italian Seasoning
    • Bayleaf
    • Chili flakes
    • Sugar

Steps

  1. Prep water in large pot. Add noodles once boiling and cook until tender.
  2. Wash and prep ALL produce
    • Dice onions and garlic
    • Slice mushrooms
  3. Add oil or butter to a separate large pot and saut√© onions, garlic, and mushrooms (salt and pepper to taste as you go)
  4. Remove veggies into side bowl then add protein to pot and cook until browned
  5. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and stewed tomatoes along with a 15oz can of water
  6. Add seasonings listed above to preference. Use sugar lightly to cut the acidty from tomatoes
  7. Add veggies back to pot and olives if desired. Mix to combine
  8. Bring to simmer then lower heat to medium-low and cover pot for 10 minutes
  9. Add parmesan cheese for topping
  10. Enjoy!
