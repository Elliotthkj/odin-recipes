Tuna Casserole

Tuna casserole is a casserole primarily made with pasta (or rice) and canned tuna, with peas and corn sometimes added.
The dish is often topped with potato chips, corn flakes, bread crumbs or canned fried onions.

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna (4 cans)
  • Cream of Mushroom (2 cans)
  • Egg noodles (or preferred substitute)
  • Milk
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Mushrooms
  • Frozen peas
  • Cheese
  • Bread or crushed potato chips

Steps

  1. Prep water in large pot. Add noodles once boiling and cook until tender.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  3. Wash and prep ALL produce
    • Dice onions and garlic.
    • Slice celery and mushrooms
  4. Add oil or butter to pan and saut√© onions, celery, mushroom, and garlic (salt and pepper to taste as you go)
  5. Once veggies are near done add peas to pan to defrost
  6. Remove veggies and add to large mixing bowl
  7. Add drained tuna, cream of mushroom, and cooked noodles to mixing bowl and slowly add up to half
    a can of milk and mix until combined
  8. In casserole dish, add all contents and spread evenly
  9. Cover top of dish with bread crumbs or crushed chips and cheese
  10. Bake at 350 degrees for about 20 minutes until cheese is melted
  11. Enjoy!
